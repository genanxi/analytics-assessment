# Business Question & Assumptions

## Business Question

What factors are associated with customer churn?

## Working Assumptions

The analysis will focus on three potential drivers of churn:

1. Product Adoption
   - Customers with lower feature adoption are more likely to churn.

2. Product Experience
   - Customers experiencing higher error rates are more likely to churn.

3. Support Experience
   - Customers with poorer support experiences are more likely to churn.
